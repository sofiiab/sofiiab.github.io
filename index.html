Stream Cookbook

There are several ingredients involved in creating the best stream possible. This website is here to help you choose your own "ingredients" to find the recipe that works for you.

Streaming Software

Open Broadcaster Software

OBS is the most popular broadcasting software used on Twitch. It's free and there are many tutorials available online.

Website

XSplit

XSplit is one of the popular alternatives to OBS. XSplit provides two different options for streamers: Broadcaster and Gamecaster.

Website

BlueStacks

Want to stream your Android games? BlueStacks has you covered.


Website

Infiniscene

Infiniscene is a cloud-based streaming software that allows you to control your stream from your browser. No downloads required.

Website

Gameshow

A $30 streaming software that promises to help you deliver broadcasts of professional quality. They also offer a free version.

Website

Tools: Alerts, Tips, and Analytics

Streamlabs

This popular tool provides alerts, donations, and a dashboard for tracking followers, subscribers, donations, and more.

Website

NightDev

The makers of BetterTwitchTV also provide a bot to help manage your chat (Nightbot), alerts, and donation services.

Website

StreamPro

StreamPro allows you to add overlays and alerts, collect donations, set goals, and take polls in chat with ease.

Website

HubTag

Hubtag has an OBS plugin that provides alerts, transitions, a live Twitter display, and more. They also have stream overlays and knowledge resources.

Website

StreamJar

StreamJar allows you to add overlays and alerts, collect donations, create custom forms, and automatically run giveaways.

Website

Stream Hatchet

Stream Hatchet is an analytical tool to help you keep track of your streaming statistics and trends.

Website

Muxy

Muxy provides alerts, analytics, and a monetization tool to connect you to sponsors. It also provides a donation service.

Website

Fan Engagement

Revlo

Revlo keeps track of how long views are watching your stream and awards them with a custom currency.

Website

StreamPerk

StreamPerk is a $14.99/month subscription service that will provide you with a website, forums, a game server, and more. You can schedule a demo on their website.

Website

Discord

Discord is a voice and text chatting application. Thanks to its Twitch integration, you can create servers to interact with subscribers.

Website

Kapsuli

Kapsuli is a social media tool that makes it easy to live-tweet your stream so your viewers know exactly when you're streaming. It also makes your stream directly viewable on Twitter.

Website

Subscriptions

Twitch Partnership

The most official way to monetize your stream on Twitch, aside from donations. You must, however, meet certain requirements and be approved by Twitch before you can get a subscribe button.

Website

Gamewisp

Gamewisp is an alternative subscription service that anyone can use. You can create personalized subscription tiers and manage subscriber benefits. Gamewisp offers Streamlabs and Discord integrations.

Website

Patreon

Patreon allows you to create a subscription service for your content. Subscriptions can be based on how much content you release or simply on a monthly basis.

Website
